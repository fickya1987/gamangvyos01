G-VYOS: Gaman Voice Your Own Search - AI Search Engine

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

G-VYOS can understand natural language queries, allowing for more intuitive and accurate search results. G-VYOS enables you to accelerate the way you gather information through multi-platform source of information such as text-voice(audio)-visual(video)-webpages-social media.

Voice Command

With the integration of voice command and Generative AI, G-VYOS will be able to assist you to search your query and respond to your voice commands and provide accurate results.

Generative AI + Real-Time Internet Data

Our G-VYOS AI search engine is constantly learning and improving, providing the most relevant search results possible.

Detail on G-VYOS

In PT Gaman Matra Kayana, we have been building and prototyping G-VYOS: Gaman Voice Your Own Search. G-VYOS is the AI (Artificial Intelligence) Web Chat & Voice (Natural Language Processing (NLP)) Based- Search Engine. G-VYOS will be expected to be released in 2023 through:
(A) Web (Web Chat)
(B) Smartphone (Web Chat)
(C) Alexa Devices: Amazon Echo Dot and Amazon Echo Show (Voice Command Audio & Visual).
